Thank you

To our instructors:

Lynn, Trevor, Sydney, Danny, Alex and Leigha: Thank you for an incredible year of growth. With your leadership and guidance, we learned skills that many of us thought were impossible to learn. It was your dedication, patience and instruction that showed us that we are capable of not only being developers but also showing us we can have the ability to be great. You showed us that determination, patience and persistence are key to coding. After the pep talks and one-on-ones, you have helped us overcome imposter syndrome and understand that we are enough. From the bottom of our hearts, thank you!



To John Wark and the NSS staff:

Thank you for the opportunity to become software developers. Coming to this school also has helped us developed as people. We have witnessed our classmates evolve into leaders and better communicators, and blossom in confidence. Ten years of your dedication has provided this to 2000-plus people. You have changed our lives, and there are not enough words to tell how much this means to us. Thank you so much!

To the career development team:

Thank you for preparing us for the hiring Tech world. Your encouragement and advice on how to network has helped us step out of our comfort zones. You have helped us market our strengths by showing us it is OK to be yourself. That not only are our skills valuable, but also who are.



To alumni:

Thank for being a supportive community. We are in awe of the alumni who are so willing to make the time to uplift students and meet with them. You have shown us that Nashville Software School graduates are a viable part of what makes this school so great.
